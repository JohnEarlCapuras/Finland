Suomenlinna Fortress
A UNESCO World Heritage site, this sea fortress is spread across six islands and offers scenic views and rich history.
Lapland
Experience the Northern Lights, reindeer safaris, and snowy wonderlands in Finland’s magical north.
Tampere
Known for its industrial history and beautiful lakeside scenery, Tampere is vibrant and youthful.
Porvoo
One of Finland’s oldest towns, Porvoo charms with cobbled streets, wooden houses, and riverside cafés.
Turku
A former capital with a medieval castle, lively cultural scene, and access to the beautiful archipelago.
Helsinki
The capital city blends modern architecture, sea views, and a chill Scandinavian lifestyle.
