Finland's Historical Journey

  • 12th–13th Century: Finland becomes part of the Swedish Kingdom.
  • 1809: Ceded to Russia; becomes the Grand Duchy of Finland.
  • 1917: Declares independence from Russia on December 6.
  • 1918: Finnish Civil War; Whites defeat Reds.
  • 1939–1940: Winter War with Soviet Union.
  • 1941–1944: Continuation War; signs armistice with USSR.
  • 1955: Joins the United Nations.
  • 1995: Joins the European Union.
  • 2023: Becomes a member of NATO.
